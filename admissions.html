Admission Requirements

To apply for admission to GBHSS Sindhri, please ensure you meet the following criteria:

  • Age Criteria: Minimum age as per government regulations for each class
  • Academic Requirements: Previous school transcripts and report cards
  • Documents: Birth certificate, B-Form/CNIC, passport-sized photographs
  • Transfer Certificate: From previous institution (for transfers)
  • Medical Fitness: Basic health declaration

Government schools follow the prescribed fee structure set by the education department.
