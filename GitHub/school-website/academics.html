Academics

Discover our comprehensive academic programs

Middle School (Class 6-8)

Our middle school program builds a strong foundation with emphasis on core subjects and developing critical thinking skills.

  • Mathematics - Building problem-solving skills
  • Science - Exploring natural phenomena
  • English - Enhancing communication skills
  • Urdu - Appreciation of literature
  • Social Studies - Understanding society and history
  • Computer Science - Introduction to technology

Matriculation (Class 9-10)

Prepare for board examinations with our structured matric program offering science and humanities streams.

Science Group

  • Physics - Laws and principles of nature
  • Chemistry - Understanding matter and reactions
  • Biology - Life sciences and ecology
  • Mathematics - Advanced problem solving

Humanities Group

  • History - Pakistan and world history
  • Geography - Earth's physical features
  • Economics - Basic economic principles
  • Civics - Government and citizenship

Intermediate (Class 11-12)

Our intermediate program offers specialized tracks for higher education preparation.

Pre-Engineering

  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Computer Science (optional)

Pre-Medical

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Computer Science (optional)

Commerce

  • Principles of Commerce
  • Accounting and Banking
  • Economics and Mathematics

General Science

  • Computer Science, Mathematics
  • Physics, Statistics

Co-Curricular Activities

Beyond academics, we offer numerous activities for holistic development:

  • Sports - Cricket, Football, Hockey, Athletics
  • Science Clubs - Research and experiments
  • Debate Society - Public speaking and argumentation
  • Literary Society - Writing and poetry
  • Scouts and Guides - Character building
  • Red Crescent - Community service
  • Music and Art - Creative expression
