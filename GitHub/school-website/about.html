About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our History

GBHSS Sindhri was established in 1965 as a beacon of education in the Sindh region.

For over 60 years, we have been committed to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, equitable, and quality education that empowers students to become lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that nurtures talent and promotes innovation.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards
  • Integrity: Building trust through honest behavior
  • Respect: Valuing every individual
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas
  • Community: Fostering belonging and responsibility
